The Employment Action Collective is dedicated to addressing the critical challenge of unemployment in our community, fostering opportunities and empowering individuals to thrive.

Understand the Challenge

Understanding the Unemployment Challenge in Our Community

A deeper look into the factors affecting employment and livelihood in our region.

Economic Hardship

Unemployment directly leads to financial instability, poverty, and reduced quality of life for families, creating a cycle of deprivation.

Mental Health Impact

Joblessness can severely affect psychological well-being, leading to increased stress, anxiety, depression, and a loss of self-worth.

Skills Gap & Education

A mismatch between available skills and evolving industry demands, along with inadequate access to relevant education, leaves many behind.

Youth Unemployment

Lack of entry-level positions, limited career guidance, and insufficient practical experience hinders the progression of our younger generation.

Our comprehensive research delves into these specific local nuances and root causes, enabling us to design targeted and effective interventions.

Our Core Pillars of Action

Strategies guiding the Employment Action Collective's impact towards a more employed community.

Job Placement & Mentorship

Connecting job seekers with relevant opportunities and providing ongoing support for successful career integration and growth.

Skills Development & Training

Offering vocational programs and workshops tailored to current and future industry demands, ensuring relevant and marketable skills.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Fostering a culture of self-employment by supporting local startups, small businesses, and innovative ventures within the community.

Economic Advocacy & Policy

Working with policymakers and stakeholders to create an environment conducive to job creation, investment, and long-term economic stability.
